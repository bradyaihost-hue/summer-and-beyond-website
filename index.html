About Hallie

I plan trips the way I wish someone had planned mine.

I've been the person who showed up to a neighborhood that looked perfect online and wasted a full day recalibrating. I've booked the most-reviewed restaurant and left wondering what the fuss was about. And after traveling internationally with a 4-month-old, I know firsthand — the right hotel room isn't a luxury, it's the difference between a good trip and a great one.

I bring taste, refinement, and hard-won judgment to every itinerary. We know what to look for — and what to avoid. Great travel comes down to discernment, not just a list. Every hour away from home is precious. I plan like I know that, because I do.

— Hallie Lenahan, Founder
