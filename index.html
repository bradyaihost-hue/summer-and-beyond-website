About Hallie

I plan trips the way I wish someone had planned mine.

Growing up, travel was how my family came together. Core memories were made while far from home. The experiences shaped me early, taking me out of my comfort zone, expanding my perspective, and even curing me of picky eating.

After traveling across nearly 50 countries, six continents and both hemispheres, I've learned one thing to be true: a great trip is not about checking boxes or following the hype. It's about discernment. Knowing what is worth your time, what only looks good online, and what will actually make a trip feel exceptional.

When I had my daughter, Summer, travel as I knew it started to look a little different. I still care about beauty, atmosphere, and discovery, but now I think just as much about ease, flow, and seamlessness. You learn quickly that with a baby, the right hotel room is more than just a luxury – it can make or break the trip. That perspective is at the heart of Summer & Beyond: inspired by Summer, designed to grow with you through every season of life.
